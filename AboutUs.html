We are eager cinephile highschool students of HASAL united in HASAL Sinema Club to share and extend our passion to other highschool students. We were established 10 years ago.Each year we organize different cinema-related events to offer teenagers a chance to experience cinema culture. Each year with the events we have organized we are growing bigger day by day. Currently we are a community of one thousand people.
